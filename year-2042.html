Penguin Learning | Lesson : Year 2042

Alice And The Rabit Hole

The year is 2042. The world is a very different place than it was just a few decades ago. Climate change has ravaged the planet, and many major cities have been abandoned due to rising sea levels. The few remaining humans live in small, isolated communities, struggling to survive in a harsh and unforgiving world. In one such community, there lives a young woman named Anya. Anya is a talented engineer, and she dreams of building a new world that is more sustainable and equitable. One day, she is working on a prototype for a new solar-powered generator when she makes a breakthrough. She realizes that her invention could revolutionize the way energy is produced, and she is determined to use it to help rebuild the world. Anya begins to travel from community to community, sharing her invention with others. She inspires people to dream big and to work together to create a better future. Slowly but surely, her message begins to spread, and hope begins to grow in the hearts of the people. One day, Anya is invited to speak at a global summit on climate change. She delivers a powerful speech, calling on the world leaders to take action to save the planet. Her words are met with cheers and applause, and she leaves the summit feeling hopeful that the future is not lost. Anya's story is a story of hope and resilience. It is a story about the power of one person to make a difference. It is a story about the importance of never giving up on our dreams.

1. What was the most important lesson that Anya learned from her journey to rebuild the world?

A. That one person can make a difference. B. That it is important to never give up on your dreams. C. That we need to work together to solve the challenges facing our planet. D. That we need to be more sustainable and equitable in our use of resources.
